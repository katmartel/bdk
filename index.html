Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a partner to attend?

No partner is necessary! Many attendees come solo, and we encourage everyone to mix and mingle during dances.

What should I wear?

Wear something comfortable that allows you to move. Dressy casual attire and clean, non-marking shoes are recommended. For men, think dress pants and dress shirts. For women, leggings, loose skirts and dresses.

What style of dance does BDK offer?

We rotate lessons between International Standard Dances (waltz, tango, quickstep, foxtrot) and International Latin Dances (cha-cha, rumba, samba, jive). During the socials, we also play songs that you can Viennese Waltz, West Coast Swing, Two Step, or Night Club Two to. There's something for everyone!

How much does it cost?

Admission is just $10 per person and includes a beginner dance lesson followed by social dancing.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely! Each event starts with a beginner-friendly lesson. No experience is needed.

When and where are the events?

Events are held monthly on Friday nights at the German Canadian Club, 1696 Cary Rd, Kelowna.
